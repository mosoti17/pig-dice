Pig Dice

Rules

  1. The goal is to reach 100 points.The first player to reach 100 points wins
  2. The players should then decide on how many times each will roll e.g 3times of 4times
  3. If a player roles 1 is turn ends and the 1 will not be added to the total
  4. When a player reaches a total of 100 or more points, the game ends and that player is the winner.
